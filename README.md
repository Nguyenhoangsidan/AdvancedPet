## 🌟 SYSTEM OVERVIEW
Are you tired of pets that just follow players around doing absolutely nothing? **AdvancedPet** is exactly what your RPG server needs!

This plugin brings pets to life by turning them into actual combat companions. They come with their own leveling system, directly buff their owner's attributes, cast powerful skills during combat, and even feature a highly addictive Gacha/Upgrade system.

---

## ✨ KEY FEATURES

🔥 **RPG Leveling System**
- Pets gain EXP alongside their owners as they fight.
- Fully customizable leveling formulas to perfectly balance your economy and progression!

⚔️ **Native Stat Scaling & Buffs**
- Pets directly add bonus stats like **Max Health**, **Movement Speed**, and **Physical Damage** to their owners!
- Flawlessly hooks into **MMOCore** & **MythicLib** for robust attribute integration.

💥 **Unleash MythicMobs Skills**
- Harness the infinite power of MythicMobs! Your pets can execute thousands of different custom skills.
- Triggers are fully customizable: Cast automatically every X seconds (Timer), on Spawn, etc.

🎲 **Addictive Upgrade & Re-roll System**
- Players earn "Upgrade Points" by leveling up their pets.
- Points can be spent in a gorgeous built-in GUI to re-roll and upgrade the pet's bonus stats. This "Gacha" style progression guarantees long-term engagement for grinders!

🖥️ **Beautiful Built-In GUIs**
- Stunning, intuitive menus for managing pets, checking active stats, and spending upgrade points.
- 100% lag-free and heavily optimized natively for your server.

🎨 **Limitless Customization**
- Apply custom textures to your pets via Base64 heads (HeadDatabase hook included).
- 100% customizable messages, GUIs, and visual effects.

## 🔗 SEAMLESS INTEGRATION
AdvancedPet was engineered from the ground up to weave flawlessly into the best premium ecosystems:
- **MythicMobs**: Drives the pet's combat behavior and custom skill logic.
- **MMOCore** / **MythicLib**: The backbone for authentic RPG stat modifications.
- **MMOItems**: Native hooks available for your native equipment systems.
- **PlaceholderAPI**: Rich placeholders (e.g., `%advancedpet_level%`, visual EXP progress bars, bonus health amount) ready for your Scoreboards, Chat, and Holograms!
- **WorldGuard**: Full PVP/Safezone protection respect so pets don't cause mayhem in spawn areas.

## 🎮 COMMANDS & PERMISSIONS

- `/pet` - Manage your available pets. *(Player access)*
- `/nangcappet` *(or `/upgrade`)* - Opens the dynamic Re-roll/Upgrade GUI.
- `/chisopet` *(or `/stats`)* - Displays the current active pet's stats.
- `/pet addexp <player> <amount>` - Admin command to grant EXP.
- **Admin Permission (`advancedpet.admin`)**: Grants full access to OP manipulation commands.
